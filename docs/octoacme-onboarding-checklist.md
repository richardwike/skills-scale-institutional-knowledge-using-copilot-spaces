# OctoAcme — Team Onboarding Checklist

## Purpose
Help new team members get up to speed on OctoAcme's project management processes, team roles, and communication norms.

> **Changelog:** Created to support onboarding clarity for the expanded set of team roles. See [issue #4](https://github.com/richardwike/skills-scale-institutional-knowledge-using-copilot-spaces/issues/4).

---

## General Onboarding (All Roles)

- [ ] Read [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md)
- [ ] Read [Roles & Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) — understand your role and how it interacts with others
- [ ] Review [Project Initiation Guide](octoacme-project-initiation.md)
- [ ] Review [Project Planning](octoacme-project-planning.md)
- [ ] Review [Execution & Tracking](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md)
- [ ] Review [Risk Management & Communication](octoacme-risks-and-communication.md)
- [ ] Attend a team standup or planning ceremony to observe team rhythm
- [ ] Meet with your direct collaborators (see role-specific section below)
- [ ] Get access to the project board and key repositories

## Role-Specific Onboarding

### Scrum Master
- [ ] Review the team's current sprint board and identify any active impediments
- [ ] Schedule introductory 1:1s with Project Manager, Product Manager, and Technical Lead
- [ ] Confirm sprint ceremony schedule (standup, planning, review, retrospective)
- [ ] Review [Retrospective & Continuous Improvement](octoacme-retrospective-and-continuous-improvement.md)

### Technical Lead
- [ ] Review current architecture documentation and any Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)
- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with Project Manager, Product Manager, and Developers
- [ ] Review open risks related to technical areas in the Risk Register
- [ ] Review [Execution & Tracking](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md) for PR and CI conventions

### UX Designer
- [ ] Review existing wireframes, prototypes, and design system/style guide
- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with Product Manager and Business Analyst
- [ ] Review current user research and usability findings
- [ ] Identify any upcoming features requiring UX design input in the backlog

### Business Analyst
- [ ] Review current requirements documentation and user stories in the backlog
- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with Product Manager, Project Manager, and Developers
- [ ] Identify any open requirements gaps or ambiguities
- [ ] Review [Project Planning](octoacme-project-planning.md) for backlog refinement process

### QA Lead
- [ ] Review the current test plan and test case library
- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with Project Manager and Technical Lead
- [ ] Review the Definition of Done and acceptance criteria for active sprint items
- [ ] Confirm CI/CD quality gates are correctly configured
- [ ] Review [Execution & Tracking](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md) for quality workflow

### Project Manager
- [ ] Review the project charter and current milestone plan
- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with Product Manager, Scrum Master, and Technical Lead
- [ ] Review the Risk Register and ensure ownership is assigned for each item
- [ ] Confirm stakeholder communication cadence is in place

### Product Manager
- [ ] Review the product roadmap and current sprint backlog
- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with Business Analyst, UX Designer, and Technical Lead
- [ ] Confirm success metrics are defined for active initiatives
- [ ] Review acceptance criteria on top-priority backlog items

### Developer
- [ ] Review the repo README and contribution guidelines
- [ ] Schedule 1:1 with Technical Lead
- [ ] Review the Definition of Done and PR review process
- [ ] Complete your first PR (or review) to validate your development environment
